More Information
Sri Venkata Ramana R&B Mill (SVRM) is a leading Manufacturer & Exporter of finest quality of Traditional brown, 1121 Steam and 1121 sella Rice in India. Established in the year 1998 in Nellore, AP(India), we are engaged in milling, processing & supply of finest qualities of Indian Rice in the form of Brown, Raw, Steam, Sella to worldwide. Our rice is rich in taste and speaks volumes in terms of purity, flavor, delectable taste & refreshing aroma. Hygienically processed and packed following industry standards, our rice is widely appreciated in the global market for their high nutritional value.
